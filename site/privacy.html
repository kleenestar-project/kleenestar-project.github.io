Privacy Policy

How KleeneStar handles your data on this website.

Last updated: 2026-06-28

1. Overview

KleeneStar is a private, non-commercial open source project. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, processed, or stored when you visit this website, and what rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

The short version: this website does not use cookies, does not set any tracking scripts, does not embed third party resources, and does not collect any personal data automatically.

2. Responsible Body

Responsible for the content of this website according to GDPR is the KleeneStar project. For contact details, see the Imprint.

3. Hosting

This website is hosted via GitHub Pages, a service operated by GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA.

When you visit this website, GitHub Pages processes the following data in server log files:

  • IP address of the requesting device
  • Date and time of the request
  • Request line and HTTP status code
  • Transferred data volume
  • User agent string of the browser

These log files are used for the stable operation of the service, for security purposes, and for statistical evaluation. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the stable and secure operation of the website).

For details on how GitHub processes data, see the GitHub Privacy Statement.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use cookies, does not write to localStorage or sessionStorage, and does not set any client side persistent identifiers.

5. Third Party Content

This website is fully self contained. It does not embed content from third parties such as Google Fonts, CDN hosted JavaScript, analytics providers, advertising networks, social media plugins, or video platforms. All assets, including fonts, styles, and images, are served directly from the same origin as the page itself.

No data is therefore transmitted to third parties when this website is loaded.

6. Contact by Email

If you contact us by email at kleenestar.project@gmail.com, we will process the data you provide (your email address, the time of receipt, and the content of your message) in order to handle your enquiry.

The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries). Your data will be deleted once your enquiry has been resolved and no statutory retention obligations prevent deletion.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details provided in the Imprint.
